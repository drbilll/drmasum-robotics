About Me

I am Dr. Md Masum Billah, a robotics and electromechanical systems specialist with over 12 years of experience in academia and industry. I specialize in robotic arm development, mechatronics, low-latency control, and assistive robotics. Currently, I lead Cambridge Robotics, where we develop intelligent and efficient robotic solutions for medical, industrial, and research applications.
